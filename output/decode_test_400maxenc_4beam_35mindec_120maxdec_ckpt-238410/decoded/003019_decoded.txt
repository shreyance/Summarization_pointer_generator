the mcc has announced the latest phase of its ` masterplan ' redevelopment of lord 's which includes the replacement of the ` tired ' tavern and allen stands at lord 's , with completion scheduled for 2019 .
the redevelopment will increase the overall capacity of lord 's , the home of english cricket , to almost 30,000 .
if the project goes ahead , a new thomas lord building and new tavern pub will also be included .
