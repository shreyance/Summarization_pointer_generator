customs officials have seized what is being described as a ` zoo in a suitcase ' from a woman accused of smuggling 108 exotic animals in russia .
in the bag , there were 55 snakes , 35 lizards , seven turtles , six lemurs , two monkeys and even a suspected leopard cub .
two baby crocodiles died on the journey from jakarta to moscow and all the survivors appeared terrified when they were found .
