militants fighting for the islamic state in syria and iraq are being led by a highly secretive group of strategists and officials .
despite thousands of foreign fighters flocking to join the sunni extremist group and starring in their propaganda videos , isis ' leadership is dominated by ex-members of the late iraqi dictator 's military .
almost all of the regional commanders appointed by isis ' leader and self-declared caliph , abu bakr al-baghdadi , played prominent roles in the baathist army before the 2003 invasion of iraq .
