tony mccoy , the greatest ever jump jockey , is guaranteed a great send-off during the grand national at aintree this week .
the 40-year-old from county antrim has been bidding his farewells as he heads for retirement and has promised to call it quits there and then then should his grand national ride shutthefrontdoor bring him a second career victory over the world 's most famous steeplechase .
mccoy is riding in his 20th grand national and aintree is busy setting the scene to make sure it is one he never forgets .
