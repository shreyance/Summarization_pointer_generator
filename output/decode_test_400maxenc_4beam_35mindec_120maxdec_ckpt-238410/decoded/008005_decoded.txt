former virginia gov. bob mcdonnell made his final written plea to a federal appeals court wednesday , arguing that the favors he did for a wealthy businessman were routine courtesies and not part of a bribery scheme .
the onetime rising republican star made the argument in a 54-page brief in the 4th u.s. circuit court of appeals court wednesday .
a three-judge panel of the court will conduct a hearing on his appeal of his public corruption convictions on may 12 .
