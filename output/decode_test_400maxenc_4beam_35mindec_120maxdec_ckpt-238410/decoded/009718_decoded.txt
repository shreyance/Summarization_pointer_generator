esa 's rosetta spacecraft has captured stunning images of a dust jet erupting from comet 67p .
the first image was taken at 7:13 am bst -lrb- 2:13 am est -rrb- on 12 march .
the jet may have been caused by a wave of heat reaching ice trapped under the surface .
but it is unusual , because so far such activity has only been spotted on the day-side of the comet .
