hull kr have plugged the gap created by the loss of front row pair michael weyman and ryan bailey with the capture of experienced australian forward dane tilse .
the 6ft 7in , 17st-plus tilse has signed a two-and-a-half-year contract with the robins and will join them as soon as he receives the necessary paperwork after the raiders agreed to release him .
tilse , 30 , has made 200 appearances down under , will link up with his old canberra team-mate terry campese .
